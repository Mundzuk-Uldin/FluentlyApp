How to Use the App

  1. Click on Start Recording and speak into your microphone.
  2. Click on Stop Recording to process your audio.
  3. Wait for the transcription and correction to appear.
  4. Select a voice from the dropdown menu.
  5. Adjust the speech rate using the slider.
  6. Click on Play Corrected Text to hear the corrected version.

If you encounter any issues, please ensure your microphone is enabled and you have a stable internet connection.
